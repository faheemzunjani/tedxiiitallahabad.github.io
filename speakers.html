HC Verma, Professor, IIT Kanpur Harish Chandra Verma is an Indian experimental physicist and professor in the Department of Physics at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India since 1994. His field of research is nuclear physics. He has authored several books on Physics at the graduate, undergraduate and school levels. His two-volume books Concepts of Physics are highly esteemed by students and Physics-lovers alike for their depth and clarity of presentation of physical concepts. He has taken initiatives like Shiksha Sopan to elevate the standard of physics education in India.He is also releasing a hindi edition of his book.
